What is BokoHacks?

BokoHacks is a web security hackathon designed for undergraduate and high school students. With a focus on both AI and cybersecurity, participants explore real-world vulnerabilities, patch security gaps, or develop new solutions—all within a fast-paced, team-based environment. It’s a chance to expand your skills and contribute to practical, modern security challenges.
